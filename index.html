About

Learn more about me

Cayleigh Ann Johnston

I am a 23 year old, internationally qualified beauty & spa therapist, part-time lecturer at Total Concept Academy (Port Elizabeth) and a model / teacher at Sophisticate Models (Port Elizabeth). I studied for 3 years at Total Concept and was awarded a bursary in my 3rd year.

In my 2nd and 3rd years at Total Concept, I achieved the top pratical marks in the university and top theory and practical marks in ITEC & CIDESCO (International qualifications).

I have had a passion for beauty since the age of 16, when I did my teacher's makeup for an event. I saw how the makeup made her feel so happy and better about herself, which meant the world to me. From that day on, I've wanted that for every client that walks through my door.

After running my own business, Beauty by Cayleigh, for the past 5 years, I am extremely proud of the progress I have made - from doing treatments on my friends just for fun, to having clients flying me out to Johannesburg and Cape Town.
